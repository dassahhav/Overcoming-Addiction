You’re not weak for struggling. You’re strong for wanting change.

Overcoming addiction is a big step, and it often requires a combination of strategies. Here are a few approaches that may help you:

  • Join a Local Gym or Boxing Club

    • Burn off stress, get stronger, stay distracted in a healthy way. You don't need to be fit to start, gym is a chill & no judgement place.

  • Hang with new people who's buzz isn't doing drugs

    • Hanging out with people who do drugs will make you want to go back into that habit, instead find other friends who don't do drugs.

  • Replace the rush

    • Many people use drugs for the “rush”, so replacing that is key. Find something you enjoy that will give you the same gives you the same excitement — whether it's hanging out with mates, learning a new skill, or even just blasting music and going for a late-night drive.

    • You don’t have to "go to rehab" or “fix everything” overnight. You just need to do something different today — even if it’s small. Do it for future you. Do it for your little siblings. Do it for your peace. You’ve got this. Real ones make change.
