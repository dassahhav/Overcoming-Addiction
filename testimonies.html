Testimonies of overcoming depression

Feeling like your struggling all alone? Well you're not alone. Whether it's stress, sadness, or just not feeling like yourself lately, you aren't alone. There's alot of people who struggle with depression. These are the people who managed to overcome depression, I hope their testimonies help you with what you're struggling with.

Debra's story

“My experience with depression started when I was a teenager, and I was a solo mum.” After becoming a young mother, Debra felt really alone. She reached out for support in her community and started medication. Over time, she was able to build resilience and take steps towards wellbeing.

Hannah's story

"Depression for me, when I think about it, it was just this absolute greyness. I could go and do something, and I had this awareness that this was a cool thing that I was doing, but it didnt feel cool. I couldnt feel that joy or that happiness."

Phillip's story

“I think what I have been through has made me more courageous. It’s made me more wholehearted, much more empathetic with and for people that find it difficult being in the world, because it’s a difficult world to be in.”
